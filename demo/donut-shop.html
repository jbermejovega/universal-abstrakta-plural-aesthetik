Why people keep coming back

It's not magic. It's butter, patience, and a fryer that's older than our lease.

Baked before sunrise

Every batch is mixed, proofed, and fried starting at 4am so it's ready when you walk in.

Local ingredients

Milk, eggs, and butter sourced from farms within 60 miles of the shop.

Never frozen, never reheated

What's in the case is what was made that morning. Nothing sits in a freezer.

Allergy-aware kitchen

Ask our staff about nut-free and dairy-free options — we label every batch clearly.
