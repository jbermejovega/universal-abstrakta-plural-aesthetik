Who We Are

Built on Grit. Driven by Passion.

Storm FC isn't just a club — it's a family. Founded in 2020, we've grown from a pickup squad into a Division I powerhouse. Our philosophy is simple: work harder than everyone else, respect the game, and lift each other up.
