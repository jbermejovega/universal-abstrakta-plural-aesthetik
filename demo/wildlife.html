Our Mission

Restoring Balance Between Humans & Nature

We believe every creature plays a vital role in the web of life. Our team works on the ground with local communities to protect endangered species, restore degraded ecosystems, and create sustainable solutions that benefit both wildlife and people.

From the rainforests of South America to the savannas of Africa, we're making a difference one habitat at a time.

Species Spotlight

Animals We Protect

Every species matters. Here are just a few of the incredible animals our conservation efforts support worldwide.

African Elephant

Listed as vulnerable, African elephants face threats from poaching and habitat loss. Our anti-poaching patrols protect over 12,000 elephants.

Learn More →

Scarlet Macaw

Deforestation has devastated macaw populations across Central America. Our reforestation projects have planted over 50,000 native trees.

Learn More →

Sea Turtle

Six of seven sea turtle species are threatened. Our coastal conservation programs protect nesting sites across 200 miles of shoreline.
