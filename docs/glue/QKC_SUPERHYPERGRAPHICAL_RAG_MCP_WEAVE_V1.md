## RAG projections

Every accepted module update should bind:

- PACAPEDIA concept entry;
- PACAPAPER mathematical or research exposition;
- PACANOTEBOOK executable/visual experiment where applicable;
- PACADEX portable PACAPDG grammar object;
- PACATERMINAL inspection and execution route;
- source, license and parent-module provenance.

## GUI/API projections

Vue, Django, Godot and other GUI/API surfaces are adapters over typed PACAPDG objects. They must not become semantic authority. PACATERMINAL is the shared SIGIL inspection/execution GUI contract; PACADEX is the portable discovery and PACAIOGAME object contract.

## Runtime boundaries

CPython, free-threaded Python, OpenMPI and OpenQASM relations remain external runtime bindings and may be marked `hardbound` only after repository-local build/test witnesses exist. Documentation alone establishes a planned or adapter-ready relation.
